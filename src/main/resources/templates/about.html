About Flattie.com

Making flat life 87% less chaotic (we ran the numbers)

Who We Are

We're a team of overcaffeinated students, former and existing flatmates, and spreadsheet-weary adults who decided: "What if flatting didn't suck?" That idea became Flattie.com — your go-to toolkit for keeping the flat running like a slightly oiled machine.

What This App Does

  • Rent Delegation Calculator: Fairly split the rent without Excel rage-quits.
  • Chore List: Assign tasks, mark them done, and eliminate mystery messes.
  • Shopping List: Share one list, so five people don't buy the same oat milk.
  • Flat Management: Create, join, and manage flats — code-based, secure, and roommate-friendly.

Why We Built It

Because every flat deserves more peace and fewer passive-aggressive sticky notes. We built Flattie to give flatmates a shared hub where fairness, clarity, and just a pinch of automation can make communal living a little more... livable.

How to Get Started

Create an account, make or join a flat, and start delegating like a flat boss. Don't worry — it's easier than assembling a flatpack shelf.
